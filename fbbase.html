Basics

This is the facebook homepage , this is where you will find your newsfeed with posts from friends and pages that you have liked, at the top of the page you will also find facebook stories, to add your own tap on the tab that says “add to story” , scroll up or down the page to see the rest of your news feed

This is the friends page , if someone has recently tried to add you on facebook they will be displayed on the top of this page, otherwise facebook will have a list of people you may know and want to, there is also an option to remove suggestions from this list

This is the facebook marketplace, here it is possible to buy and sell items from other facebook users, at the top of the page there is a tab of categories that you can look through, otherwise click the magnifying glass icon to search for particular things

This is your profile, by tapping on your profile picture or background picture you are prompted to change them, this is also where you will see your own details as well as posts that you have made, or posts you have been tagged in

This is the notification page, here you will find notifications for events that are happening near you, pages that you like or about posts you have been tagged in or interacted with

This is the options page where you can find direct links to other parts of facebook , by scrolling to the bottom of this page you can log out or access your private settings
