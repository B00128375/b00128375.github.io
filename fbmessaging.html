Messaging

Age rating 13

Facebook will be extremely useful to lecturers and students as it has multiple options for communicating, as well as allowing users to post all sorts of formats, lecturers can create group pages to share lecture content, the messenger app is also a handy add-on as it can display messages without having to go into the app
