Basics

This is the snapchat home, press the highlighted button to take a photo, you will then be able to post this photo on your story or send it to friends

Press this button to flip the camera, to make it face inwards instead of outward (or vice versa)

This is the story page , at the top of the screen you will see your friends stories, followed by your subscriptions (pages which you follow) and then further down will be random page stories.

By pressing on the head icon at the top of the homescreen , you will be brought onto your snapchat page, by pressing the highlighted buttons , you can create a story or a private story (your choice of who can see this story)

This is your chat page , here you can see your friends and group chats , you can tap on any of these to send a message or picture
