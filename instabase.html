Basics

This is the Instagram homepage , here you can see your news feed , stories and access messages

Press the “your story” button to create a new story

Press any of the icons along the story bar to see that persons story

Press the magnifying glass icon to go to the search page , here you will see recommended pages as well as a search bar to find other pages you like

Press the plus button to add a new post on your own instagram page

Press the heart to access your like page where you can see your recent post likes and comments as well as recent followers

Press the heart icon below posts to like them, use the chat bubble icon to leave a comment and use the arrow to send a post to someone as a message.

Press the profile icon to access your page where you can see your posts , followers and following as well as accessing your account settings through the gear icon
