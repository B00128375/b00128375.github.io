Basics

This is your chat page , here you will be able to enter any chat you have previously been in, press the green message icon in the bottom right corner to create a new chat with any of your contacts

This is your status page, you can tap on your profile icon to add a status update which your contacts can see, you can press the pen icon to write a status or the camera icon to take a photo

This is the calls page , here you will find a call log for your whatsapp, press the green phone icon in the bottom right corner to start a call
